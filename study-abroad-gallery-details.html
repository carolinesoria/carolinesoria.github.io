Study Abroad

When studying in Aix-en-Provence, France as an undergraduate student, I had the chance to visit some memorable places:

  • Amazing architecture at the entrance to the Bibliothèque Méjanes, a public library
  • Creepy catacombs beneath Paris, lined with ornately-designed stacks of old bones
  • The historic city of Marseille along the Mediterranean coast, a short bus ride away
  • Ancient ruins of Pompeii and the looming Vesuvius volcano near Naples, Italy
